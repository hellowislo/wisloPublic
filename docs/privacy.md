# Wislo Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 12, 2025

Wislo is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

## Data We Collect
- **App Usage Data**: Tracks time spent on apps to help parents monitor their child’s screen time.
- **Accessibility Events**: Detects app launches to display questionnaires for safe usage.
- **Device ID**: A unique identifier to securely associate profiles with devices. This data is encrypted and not shared with third parties.

## Why We Need Permissions
- **Usage Access**: To monitor app usage time effectively.
- **Accessibility Service**: To detect app launches and show questionnaires.
- **Notifications**: To send reminders and alerts about your child’s activities.

## Data Security
We use encryption to protect your data and do not share it with third parties except as required by law.

## Contact Us
For privacy inquiries, contact us at support@wislo.app.
